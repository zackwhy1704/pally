## Full Description

### Hook (first three lines — the differentiator)
Every tutor knows a textbook. Pally knows **yours**.

Upload your class notes, slides, or past papers, and Pally builds a personal
AI tutor that answers from your exact syllabus — not some generic internet
article that might not match what your teacher taught.

### What makes Pally different

**Trained on your material, not a generic syllabus.**  
You teach Pally your notes. It learns your textbook, your topics, your
curriculum. So when you ask a question, the answer actually matches your
class — not a stranger's textbook.

**Remembers how you learn.**  
When you get something wrong, Pally notices — and brings it back until it
clicks. Topics you already know get spaced out. No time wasted; no topic
left behind.

**Real study depth.**  
Not a do-everything AI. A precision study tool:
- 📚 Subject-specialist tutors built from your uploaded notes
- 🎯 Daily quizzes calibrated to your level
- 🔁 Spaced-repetition flashcards (SM-2 algorithm)
- 📈 Mastery tracking by topic
- 🔒 Curriculum-aligned: PSLE → O-Level → A-Level → professional
- 👨‍👩‍👧 Parent dashboard with safety oversight

### Who it's for
Students preparing for PSLE, O-Level, A-Level, IGCSE, IB, and beyond.
Perfect for Singapore's curriculum — and any school that uses a textbook.

### Privacy & safety
- Parental consent required for under-13 learners (PDPA-compliant)
- Notes are never sold and never used to train AI models
- Content moderation runs on every conversation
- One-tap data deletion in Settings
